# 09. Copy Deck and Microcopy

## Tone of voice
- professional
- calm
- precise
- confident without overclaiming
- direct

## UI naming

### Preferred labels
- New matter
- Consultation
- Analysis
- Evidence
- Authorities
- Related cases
- Risk alerts
- Draft answer
- Verified sources
- Graph explorer
- Matter status

### Avoid
- Magic
- Genius AI
- Smartest
- Ask anything casually in serious panels

## Example empty state copy

### Workspace
**No matters yet**  
Start a new matter to analyze a legal question, inspect authorities, and build a grounded response.

### Consultation
**Start with a legal question**  
Describe the situation in plain language. The system will identify issues, retrieve authorities, and draft a structured response.

### Evidence
**No evidence selected**  
Choose a citation or source to inspect supporting text and metadata.

### Graph
**No node selected**  
Search for an entity, issue, law, or case to explore related authorities and connections.
